## 🧩 DAX

- Revenue Previous Month = CALCULATE(sum(Case_Sample_Data_Set[NetRevenue]),DATEADD('Calendar'[Month], -1, MONTH))
- Revenue MoM Change = [Total NetRevenue] - [Revenue Previous Month]
- Revenue Previous Month by Customer = CALCULATE(SUM(Case_Sample_Data_Set[NetRevenue]),DATEADD('Calendar'[Month], -1, MONTH),
    ALLEXCEPT(Case_Sample_Data_Set,Case_Sample_Data_Set[CustomerName]))
- Revenue MoM % Change = DIVIDE([Total NetRevenue] - [Revenue Previous Month],[Revenue Previous Month])
-  Revenue MoM Display = VAR Arrow = IF([Revenue MoM % Change] >= 0, "▲ ", "▼ ")
   RETURN Arrow & FORMAT([Revenue MoM % Change] * 100, "0.0") & " p.p."

## 📈 Some Insights Found
 #### 📌 Some of the key insights identified from the dashboards analysis are outlined below. Total revenue showed a slight decline of 1.1 percentage points in January compared to December. The Asia-Pacific, Europe, and South America regions experienced decreases, with Asia-Pacific showing the largest decline at 6.6 percentage points.
 #### 📌 From a segment perspective, Enterprise, Small Business, and Wholesale segments also declined compared to the previous month.
 #### 📌 On the Monthly Revenue by Region analysis page, although Asia-Pacific recorded the largest decrease compared to December, revenue volume remained higher than in October and November, among other notable trends identified in the dataset.
#### 📌 On the Segments analysis page, Retail delivered the strongest positive performance, becoming the segment with the highest revenue and representing 23% of total monthly revenue. The highest monthly revenue was generated by the customer Smith LLC, as shown in the table.
